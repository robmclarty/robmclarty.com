Hi, I'm Rob. I like playing with computers, riding my bike, drawing pictures, reading philosophy, and dreaming about one day finishing a project!

I live downtown by myself and work for a startup, sitting in various ajustable chairs, pressing various plastic buttons, and reading a lot of of the same thing over and over again. I want to help people protect themselves from the network.

I wonder a lot about the absurdity of conscious existence and grapple with the phenomena of Being whilst playing the role society has set for me—whilst trying not to think about any of it too hard—in an effort to find some sense of balance.

I don't make friends very easily. I feel like most people I know only superficially. But one thing this pandemic is teaching me is that there is only now, and there's no time to waste holding back one's truth.
