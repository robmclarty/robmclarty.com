Co-op Cabs

Online taxi ordering system that connects directly to Co-op's computerized dispatch system + public marketing website with custom integrated CMS.

Launch Website

In order to offer its customers the most convenient options for ordering a taxi, Co-op needed a responsive web application that worked on multiple platforms. Developed in Rails with heavy use of Javascript we developed custom-integrated CMS, blog, rating, and messaging systems, along with a responsive, multi-device, online cab ordering system. Customers are able to quickly fill out a simple form, geolocate themselves (or enter their desired pickup location manually), and place an order directly to Co-op's computer-controlled dispatch system. Once an order is placed, real-time status updates are provided to customers so they know where their assigned cab is on a map relative to their pickup location, eventually notifying them when it's time to go outside and hop in.

Verold

Interactive 3D for everyone. Verold Studio is a powerful online visual editor that lets you turn 3D content into interactive presentations on the web.

Demo Videos   (web platform is no longer active)

Leveraging many bleeding-edge technologies, Verold enables users to transform their 3D content into online interactive presentations, apps, showcases, and more. Based on WebGL, the Verold embed has enabled artists, educators, marketers, and business people to better connect to their audiences through greater immersion and interaction on any device with an internet connection.

Essentially what's happening is this:
  1. bring some 3D stuff (any format)
  2. turn it into Verold™ 3D stuff (processed behind the scenes when you upload your 3D stuff)
  3. compose a scene out a library of Verold™ 3D stuff (your own or shared by the community)
  4. share it with the world using Verold's embed widget
  5. or integrate it with your web app directly using Verold's API
  6. enjoy amazing interactive 3D content rendered in the highest quality possible with WebGL using Verold's custom 3D rendering engine (based on Three.js)
